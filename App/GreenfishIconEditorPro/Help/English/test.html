How to...

Test your icon, cursor or animation

  1. Press Shift+Ctrl+T or choose Icon|Test.

    By default, an image with four gray levels appears in a popup window.

  2. Move the mouse over the image to see how your icon or cursor looks over different backgrounds, or preview your animation.
  3. If trying out a cursor, click and drag to test the hot spot.

    This will draw a red freehand curve over the background.

  4. Click the folder button to load a custom background for the test site.
